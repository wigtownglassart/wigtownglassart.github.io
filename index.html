Lynn Morley - Stained Glass Artist

Based in Wigtown, Dumfries and Galloway, and inspired by the stunning scenery of the local landscape, Lynn's stained glass artwork ranges from small shelf pieces to larger commissioned leaded or copper foiled panels. Her popular 'Galloway Hills' range of tea-lights reflect the colours and hues of the local area. The 'Seascape' pieces take their colour palette from the local beaches. Lynn's glass artwork often incorporates sea glass, agate or ammonites allowing the beauty of the glass to be shown to best effect. Lynn copper foils the glass and uses the soldered lines as part of the artwork, to reflect light and give the piece a more contemporary feel. Larger pieces are leaded using traditional methods

Smaller pieces of glass are used to construct mosaic pictures. This method is particularly effect for depicting birds in their local habitat.

Lynn makes small pieces as shelf art, larger framed panels which are mounted in renovated fire-screens, as unique and personal pieces of artwork, as commissions or for sale through local venues and events.

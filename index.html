Lynn Morley - Stained Glass Artist

Working from Roberton, in the Upper Clyde Valley, I design and make stained glass copper foiled artwork, ranging from small shelf items to larger panels. These are set in vintage firescreen frames, to provide freestanding panels. The richness of colour in stained glass is an important influence in my work. My designs are simple with strong lines and proportions, allowing the glass to be shown to its true potential The work has a contemporary feel. I am happy to undertake commissions.
